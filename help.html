How to play

  • Fill in the registration form
  • Agree with the Terms and Conditions
  • Press PLAY

Find matching pairs

  1. The goal is to find matching pairs of cards with identical AMUSNET slots pictures.
  2. Remember the pictures on each card as you flip them over. Pay attention to the locations of the cards you've flipped over, as this information becomes crucial for making matches based on your memory.
  3. If the cards match, they will remain face-up.
  4. If the cards do not match, they will flip back face-down, and you try again to find another match.

Complete the game

  1. Continue playing until you have successfully matched all the pairs.
  2. The game will display the time taken to complete the game.
  3. After finishing, check the Leaderboard to see your ranking.
  4. If you have secured a spot among the top 10 players for the day, you participate in a lucky draw.
