    1. Participant in the Raffle is any natural person who meets the condition under item 4.1., is not a person under item 4.3 and who has registered and fulfilled the requirements according to the terms and conditions specified in these Rules.
    2. The person who wishes to register to participate in the Raffle provides Amusnet Interactive with the following data necessary for registration:

      b. electronic mail (e-mail)
      c. company of employment
    3. To participate in the raffle, the person should:
      1) fill out an online participation form, entering the data specified in item 5.2. and give her/his consent to participate in the Raffle by clicking the "I Agree" button, and;
      2) play the Amusnet Memory Cards Game (hereinafter referred to as the "Game"), as described under item 5.4.;
      3) steps 1) and 2) can be executed either in person on the TABS multimedia table available at the relevant booth of Amusnet Interactive, or by scanning the QR code on the promotional flyers for the Raffle, either on the person's personal device or on a tablet provided by the hostesses at Amusnet Interactive's booth.
    4. To participate in the Game, the person should follow the guidelines below:
      a. The goal of the Game is to find matching pairs of cards with identical symbols from Amusnet Interactive's slot games for the shortest amount of time.
      b. If the cards match, they will remain face-up. If the cards do not match, they will flip back face-down, and you try again to find another match.
      c. The person participating in the Game should pay attention to the locations of the cards once flipped over, as this information becomes crucial for making matches based on memory.
      d. To complete the Game the person must successfully match all the pairs of cards. The time taken to complete the Game will then be displayed.
      e. After finishing, a Leaderboard will display the participant's ranking. The Leaderboard will include the name and surname of the current Top 10 participants, as well as the exact time they completed the Game that ranked them at the relevant spot on the Leaderboard.
      f. If a person has secured a spot among the Top 10 players for the day, he/she participates in a draw for a chance to win one of the prizes.
      g. A person can play the Game multiple times in order to improve his/her ranking or simply visit the Leaderboard to check the current ranking.
